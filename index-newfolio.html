games4girlz.com

Illustrations for Voiceworks magazine non-fiction piece 'games4girlz.com', for online issue 'Cipher'. The essay, coded and written by Erin Moss, can be read both linearly and non-linearly, so that you can play and read the piece in whatever order you choose. The essay, format and illustrations reflect on girl's dress-up flash games of the 2000's. Inspirations for the illustrations included Barbie, MyScene, Bratz, and the Sims, as well as current-day dress-up games for kids. 2022.
