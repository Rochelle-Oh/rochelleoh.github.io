Let's Talk About Consent
Animation, 2018.
This frame-by-frame animation was created to breakdown the relationship and stigma between sex and consent – in a fun, non-gender-specific way.

This animation has been praised for its quality and delivery of information by Monash University’s Diversity & Inclusion team and was selected to be used in the university RESPECT. NOW. ALWAYS. campaign. It is also used as educational material in staff and leadership training.

Supervised by Jess Kerss.
