🐟 Rochelle Oh is a 21-year-old graphic designer, illustrator, animator, photographer and content creator from Aotearoa (New Zealand), working in in 📌Naarm (Melbourne).

This website was created on the unceded lands of the Wurundjeri people of the Kulin Nation. My work could not exist without their continued connection and contribution to land, water and community. I pay my respects to Elders past, present and emerging, and extend my respects to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples. This always was, always will be, Aboriginal land.
I extend my respects to all First Nations communities around the world.
