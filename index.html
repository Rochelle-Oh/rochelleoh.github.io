🐟 Rochelle Oh is a Naarm (Melbourne) based graphic designer and illustrator. I’m currently designing record covers, merch, social media assets and more for Bedroom Suck Records and Music In Exile. I love working with other creatives across disciplines and challenging my skills.

This website was created on unceded Woiworung & Boonwurrung lands of the Kulin nation. My work could not exist without their continued connection and contribution to land, water and community. I pay my respects to Elders past, present and emerging, and extend my respects to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples. This always was, always will be, Aboriginal land.
